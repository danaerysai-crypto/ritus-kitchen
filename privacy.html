Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. What we collect

When you place an order through our website or WhatsApp, we collect your name, phone number, delivery area, meal preference, dietary needs, and how you heard about us. This information is needed only to prepare and deliver your food.

2. How we use your data

We use your details to confirm your order, communicate delivery updates, keep a record of your preferences, and improve our menu. We do not sell your data to anyone.

3. Where your data is stored

Order information is stored in a private Google Sheet linked to our order form. Only Ritu and authorised helpers can access it.

4. Cookies and analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website — for example, which pages are most viewed. This data is anonymous and does not identify you personally.

5. WhatsApp communication

Clicking our WhatsApp button opens a chat with us on your device. WhatsApp's own privacy policy applies to messages sent through their platform.

6. Your rights

You can ask us to update or delete your information at any time. Email us at rituskitchen.av@gmail.com or message us on WhatsApp.
