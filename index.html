Hi, I'm Ritu.

I've always loved cooking for family and friends — and also my dog. It's how I show care: through a hot phulka, a bowl of dal, a wholesome nutritious meal.

A few months ago I went gluten-free, not for trend, but because my body asked me to. That changed how I cook. I became mindful of ingredients in a way I never was before. I started experimenting — jowar rotis that puff, coconut-milk paysum that tastes like childhood, cakes without wheat that still feel like celebration.

My promise is simple: fresh, seasonal food with no shortcuts. Every meal has vegan and gluten-free options — just tell me what you need when you book.

— Ritika Kukreja
