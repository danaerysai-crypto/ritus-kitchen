# Customer Feedback + Dietary Survey Form

Use Google Forms (free) and link responses to Google Sheets.

## Form Questions

### 1. Your Name
- Short answer, required

### 2. Phone Number / WhatsApp
- Short answer, required

### 3. Where are you staying in Auroville?
- Multiple choice:
  - Guesthouse
  - Long-term resident
  - Volunteer house
  - Yoga center
  - Nearby (Pondicherry)
  - Other

### 4. Are you a visitor, volunteer, or long-term Aurovilian?
- Multiple choice:
  - Visitor / tourist
  - Volunteer
  - Newcomer (first 0–6 months)
  - Long-term resident
  - Other

### 5. How did you hear about Ritu's Kitchen?
- Multiple choice:
  - WhatsApp group
  - Instagram
  - Google search
  - Friend / referral
  - Guesthouse
  - Auroville notice board
  - Auroville Today / Radio
  - Other

### 6. What meals do you need?
- Checkboxes:
  - Lunch
  - Dinner
  - Both
  - Weekly tiffin subscription
  - One-time order

### 7. Any dietary preferences or restrictions?
- Checkboxes:
  - Vegetarian
  - Vegan
  - Gluten-free
  - No onion-garlic (sattvic)
  - Low-oil
  - Diabetic-friendly
  - Mild spice
  - No coconut
  - Allergies (please specify)
  - Other (short answer)

### 8. How many people is this meal for?
- Multiple choice:
  - Just me
  - 2 people
  - 3–4 people
  - 5+ people

### 9. What is most important to you in a tiffin service?
- Checkboxes:
  - Taste
  - Freshness
  - Price
  - Delivery reliability
  - Customization
  - Hygiene
  - Consistent timing

### 10. How likely are you to order again?
- Linear scale 1–5

### 11. What would make you recommend Ritu's Kitchen?
- Paragraph answer

### 12. Any other feedback or special request?
- Paragraph answer

| Field | Action |
|-------|--------|
| Phone | Add to WhatsApp broadcast list |
| Area | Optimize delivery route |
| Dietary restrictions | Create custom meal options |
| Referral source | Double down on top channels |
| Likely to order again | Identify loyal customers |
| Feedback | Fix issues + use as testimonials |

---

## Google Form Setup Steps

1. Go to `forms.google.com`
2. Create blank form → title: "Ritu's Kitchen — Feedback"
3. Add questions above
4. Click **Responses tab** → **Link to Sheets**
5. Choose "Create a new spreadsheet"
6. Share form link in WhatsApp bio, Instagram bio, and after first order
