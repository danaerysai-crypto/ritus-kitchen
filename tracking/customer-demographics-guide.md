# Ritu's Kitchen — Important Customer Demographics to Capture

## Why Demographics Matter

Demographics turn guesses into decisions. For Ritu's Kitchen, knowing **who** orders, **where** they live, **why** they choose you, and **how often** they return helps you:

- Focus marketing on high-value channels
- Design the right products (thali types, sizes, subscriptions)
- Optimize delivery routes and timing
- Increase repeat orders and lifetime value
- Improve pricing and profitability

---

## Tier 1: Must Capture on Every Order

These are non-negotiable. Log them every time, even for WhatsApp orders.

| Field | Why It Matters | How to Capture |
|-------|----------------|----------------|
| **Name** | Personalize communication | Ask during first order |
| **Phone / WhatsApp** | Core channel for repeat orders and broadcasts | Save in phone contacts |
| **Order date** | Track seasonality, demand patterns | Order log |
| **Product ordered** | Know what sells | Order log |
| **Quantity** | Measure volume | Order log |
| **Price paid** | Revenue analysis | Order log |
| **Payment mode** | Cash flow, UPI vs cash | Order log |
| **Delivery area / location** | Route planning, demand clusters | Ask during order |
| **Source / how they heard about you** | Marketing attribution | Ask explicitly |

---

## Tier 2: Capture Within First 3 Orders

Capture these through casual WhatsApp chat or the feedback form.

| Field | Why It Matters | Question to Ask |
|-------|----------------|-----------------|
| **Resident type** | Tourists, newcomers, long-term residents, volunteers have different value | "Are you a visitor, volunteer, or long-term Aurovilian?" |
| **Accommodation type** | Guesthouse, house, yoga center, farm | "Where are you staying?" |
| **Meal need** | Lunch, dinner, both, subscription intent | "Do you need lunch, dinner, or both?" |
| **Frequency intent** | Daily, weekly, occasional | "Are you looking for a one-time meal or a regular tiffin?" |
| **Dietary restrictions** | Vegan, gluten-free, no onion-garlic, diabetic, low-oil, no spice | "Any dietary needs Ritu should know?" |
| **Family size / who eats** | Single, couple, family with kids | "Is this just for you or for family too?" |
| **Price sensitivity** | Budget, value, premium willing | Observe by what they order; ask "What price range works for you?" |
| **Occasion** | Regular meal, special event, festival, retreat | "Is this for daily eating or a special occasion?" |

## Tier 3: Capture Over Time for Strategy

These help with long-term marketing and product development.

| Field | Why It Matters | How to Capture |
|-------|----------------|----------------|
| **Discovery channel** | Instagram, WhatsApp group, Google, friend, guesthouse, notice board | Always log source |
| **Referrer name** | Identify advocates and reward them | "Who referred you?" |
| **Repeat vs new** | Measure loyalty and retention | Compare order dates |
| **Total orders to date** | Customer lifetime value | Order log calculation |
| **Total spent to date** | Revenue per customer | Order log calculation |
| **Average order value** | Identify high-value customers | Order log calculation |
| **First order date** | Customer age / cohort analysis | Order log |
| **Last order date** | Win-back campaigns | Order log |
| **Days between orders** | Purchase frequency | Order log calculation |
| **Preferred order time** | Optimize broadcast timing | Ask or observe |
| **Preferred delivery time** | Lunch ~1 PM, dinner ~7 PM | Ask during order |
| **Feedback / satisfaction** | Fix issues, use testimonials | Google Form or WhatsApp |
| **Likelihood to recommend** | NPS-style loyalty signal | Google Form scale 1–5 |
| **Special occasions ordered for** | Festival boxes, event catering | Order notes |
| **Preferred cuisine type** | North Indian, South Indian, fusion | Ask during menu discussion |
| **Spice tolerance** | Mild, medium, spicy | Ask during first order |
| **Allergies** | Health and safety | Ask explicitly |
| **Language preference** | English, French, Tamil, Hindi | Note for communication |
| **Occupation / role in Auroville** | Karma yoga, visitor, staff, yoga student | Helps segment offers |

## Demographic Segments to Track

Build a simple segmentation in Excel:

| Segment | Definition | Marketing Action |
|---------|------------|--------------------|
| **Newcomers (0–6 months)** | First-time in Auroville | Newcomer starter pack offer |
| **Regulars** | Order ≥3 times | Loyalty rewards, early access |
| **Subscribers** | Weekly tiffin customers | Retention priority |
| **Wellness customers** | Vegan/gluten-free/low-oil | Wellness thali promotion |
| **Families** | Orders for 2+ people | Family thali upsell |
| **Referrers** | Brought new customers | Referral bonus program |
| **Dormant** | No order in 30+ days | Win-back message |
| **Event buyers** | Festival/catering orders | Pre-event campaign |

## How to Capture Without Annoying Customers

### First order (WhatsApp)

> "Hi! This is Ritu's Kitchen. Thanks for your order. Just to serve you better:
> 1. Where should we deliver?
> 2. Any dietary needs?
> 3. How did you hear about us?"

### After delivery (WhatsApp)

> "Hope you enjoyed your thali! 🍛 A quick feedback form (1 minute) helps us improve: [Google Form link]. Thank you!"

### Monthly check-in (for regulars)

> "Hi [Name], you've ordered [X] times with us! Is there anything you'd like us to add to the menu?"

## Updated Customer Survey Questions (Prioritized)

Use these 8 questions maximum. More than 8 reduces response rate.

1. **Name**
2. **Phone / WhatsApp**
3. **Where are you staying in Auroville?** (guesthouse / resident / volunteer / yoga center / Pondicherry)
4. **How did you hear about Ritu's Kitchen?**
5. **What meals do you need?** (lunch / dinner / both / weekly tiffin)
6. **Any dietary restrictions?** (vegan / gluten-free / no onion-garlic / low-oil / diabetic / mild spice / allergies)
7. **How likely are you to order again?** (1–5 scale)
8. **What would make you recommend us?** (open text)

## Key Analysis Questions Demographics Will Answer

| Question | Action from Answer |
|----------|--------------------|
| Which area has the most customers? | Optimize delivery route and marketing there |
| Which source brings the highest-value customers? | Spend more time/money there |
| What percentage become weekly subscribers? | Focus onboarding funnel on subscription |
| Which dietary need is most common? | Add a dedicated product or menu option |
| What is the average customer lifetime value? | Set acquisition cost limit |
| How long before a customer reorders? | Send reminder at the right time |
| Who refers the most new customers? | Reward them and ask for more |

Keep this as a note on your phone or pinned in WhatsApp Business:

```
Every new order, capture:
- Name
- Phone
- Area
- Meal need (lunch/dinner/subscription)
- Dietary needs
- Source (how they found us)
- Resident type (visitor/resident/volunteer)

Every repeat order, update:
- Total orders
- Total spent
- Last order date
- Days between orders
```
