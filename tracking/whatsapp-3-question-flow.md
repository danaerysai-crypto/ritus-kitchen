# WhatsApp 3-Question Order Flow
## Capture Customer Data Naturally

---

## The Core Idea

Every new customer gives you their profile **while ordering**. No forms. No surveys. Just a normal WhatsApp conversation.

---

## New Customer Conversation Flow

### Customer:
> Hi, I want to order today's thali.

### You (Ritu's Kitchen):
> Hi! 🍛 I'd love to serve you.
> 
003e **Where should I send today's thali?**
> > (Your area / guesthouse name)

---

### Customer:
> Verite Guesthouse

### You:
> Perfect. **Any dietary needs?** Vegan, gluten-free, no onion-garlic, mild spice?
> > (Or just say "regular" if none)

---

### Customer:
> Mild spice please

### You:
> Noted. One mild thali to Verite Guesthouse — ₹220.
> 
003e **One last thing — how did you hear about us?** WhatsApp group, Instagram, a friend, guesthouse, or Google?

---

### Customer:
> My friend Priya

### You:
> Thank you Priya's friend! 🙏 Your thali will be delivered around 1 PM.
> 
003e UPI / Google Pay: +91 90431 40686

---

## Quick Reference: 3 Questions to Ask

| # | Question | Captures |
|---|----------|----------|
| 1 | Where should I send it? | Area, accommodation |
| 2 | Any dietary needs? | Vegan, GF, no onion-garlic, mild spice, allergies |
| 3 | How did you hear about us? | Marketing source |

---

## Pro Tips

- Save their address and dietary need after first order
- Use WhatsApp labels: 🟢 New, 🔵 Regular, 🌿 Wellness
- For repeat customers, confirm — don't re-ask
- If they hesitate on source, say "No problem, just helps us know what's working"
- Never ask all 3 questions in one message. One at a time, in flow.
