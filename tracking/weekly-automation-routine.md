# Ritu's Kitchen — Weekly Automation Routine
## ~2 Hours Per Week Total

---

## Sunday: The Big 1 Hour

### 10:00 AM — Schedule Social Media (45 min)
- [ ] Open Meta Business Suite
- [ ] Upload 7 photos/videos
- [ ] Copy captions from `social-media-captions-30-days.md`
- [ ] Add UTM links to each caption
- [ ] Schedule one post per day
- [ ] Schedule 2 Instagram Stories per day

### 10:45 AM — Review Dashboard (15 min)
- [ ] Open `ritus-kitchen-marketing-dashboard.xlsx`
- [ ] Update orders from the past week
- [ ] Note top source and top product
- [ ] Decide next week's experiment

---

## Daily: 15 Minutes Total

### 8:00 AM — Morning WhatsApp Status (5 min)
- [ ] Take photo of today's thali
- [ ] Post with caption: "Today's thali — ₹220. Pre-book by 11 AM. [WhatsApp link]"

### 1:00 PM — Confirm Orders + Thanks (10 min)
- [ ] Confirm any pending WhatsApp orders
- [ ] Send `/thanks` quick reply to delivered orders
- [ ] Send `/review` quick reply to happy repeat customers

### 7:00 PM — Tomorrow's Menu Status (5 min)
- [ ] Post tomorrow's menu photo/status
- [ ] Encourage weekly tiffin subscriptions

---

## Weekly: Retention Tasks (15 min)

### Sunday 12:00 PM — Dormant Win-Back
- [ ] Filter customers with no order in 30+ days
- [ ] Send `/winback` message to each
- [ ] Log responses in dashboard

### Wednesday — Mid-Week Check
- [ ] Check which posts got most engagement
- [ ] Boost the winning format with an extra story

---

## Monthly: 30 Minutes

### Last Sunday of Month
- [ ] Update `Monthly KPI Dashboard` sheet
- [ ] Compare month-over-month growth
- [ ] Review pricing and profitability
- [ ] Plan next month's content themes
- [ ] Update Google Business Profile photos and posts

## Quick Replies to Set Up in WhatsApp Business

| Shortcut | Message |
|----------|---------|
| `/menu` | "Today's thali: [menu]. ₹220. Pre-book by 11 AM." |
| `/order` | "Order here: [order.html link] or reply with your name, area, and meal need." |
| `/thanks` | "Hope you enjoyed your thali! 🍛 Quick 30-sec feedback? [link]" |
| `/review` | "If you loved the food, a Google review helps more Aurovilians find us. [link] 🙏" |
| `/winback` | "Hi [Name], we miss serving you! 🍛 Want to book a thali this week? Reply yes and I'll save one for you." |
| `/tiffin` | "Weekly tiffin — 5 meals for ₹1,100. No daily ordering. Subscribe? [link]" |

## The 2-Hour Promise

After setup, you spend only:
- **1 hour on Sunday** scheduling + reviewing
- **15 minutes daily** on WhatsApp status + confirmations
- **15 minutes weekly** on win-backs

The rest runs itself: order form, data logging, analytics, social posts, and customer follow-ups.
