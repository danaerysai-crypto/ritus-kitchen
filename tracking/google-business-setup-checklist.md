# Ritu's Kitchen — Google Business Profile Setup Checklist

## Why This Matters
Google Business Profile is **free local advertising**. When people search "home food Auroville", "tiffin service Pondicherry", or "Indian food near me", your listing can appear at the top.

---

## Phase 1: Create / Claim Your Profile

- [ ] Go to `https://business.google.com`
- [ ] Sign in with a Google account (create one if needed)
- [ ] Click "Manage now" or "Add your business to Google"
- [ ] Enter business name: **Ritu's Kitchen**
- [ ] Choose category:
  - Primary: **Meal Delivery** or **Home Meal Delivery**
  - Secondary: **Indian Restaurant**, **Vegetarian Restaurant**
- [ ] Add address or service area:
  - If you don't want to show home address, choose **Service area business**
  - Add areas: **Auroville, Pondicherry, Tamil Nadu**
- [ ] Add phone number: **+91 90431 40686**
- [ ] Add email: **rituskitchen.av@gmail.com**
- [ ] Add website: `https://ritus-kitchen.vercel.app`
- [ ] Verify via postcard, phone, or email (Google will offer available options)

---

## Phase 2: Complete Your Profile

- [ ] **Description** (750 characters max):
  > "Ritu's Kitchen serves home-cooked South Indian thalis in Auroville. Fresh, made-to-order meals with vegan, gluten-free, and no onion-garlic options. Pre-booking only. Weekly tiffin subscriptions available."

- [ ] **Hours:** Set based on your actual meal prep/delivery hours

---

## Phase 3: Weekly Activity (15 min/week)

- [ ] **Post once every 2–3 days** on Google Business
  - Use photo + 1–2 sentences + WhatsApp link
  - Examples:
    - "Today's thali: [menu]. Pre-book by 11 AM."
    - "Weekly tiffin — 5 meals for ₹1,100. Subscribe today."
    - "Wellness thali: vegan, gluten-free, low-oil options available."

- [ ] **Add photo every week**
  - Fresh thali photo keeps profile active

- [ ] **Reply to all reviews** within 24 hours
  - Thank positive reviewers
  - Address negative reviews politely

- [ ] **Answer Q&A promptly**
  - People ask delivery area, price, dietary options

---

## Phase 4: Ask for Reviews

### Who to ask
- Happy repeat customers
- Newcomers who loved the food
- Guesthouse managers
- Yoga center contacts

### How to ask (WhatsApp message)
> Hi [Name], thank you for ordering from Ritu's Kitchen! If you have a moment, a Google review really helps other Aurovilians discover home-cooked food. 🙏
> 
> [Google review link]

### Create your review link
1. Go to `https://business.google.com`
2. Click "Ask for reviews"
3. Copy your review link
4. Save it in WhatsApp quick replies

---

## Phase 5: Track Performance

In Google Business Profile Insights, watch:

| Metric | What It Tells You |
|--------|-------------------|
| **Search views** | How often you appear in search |
| **Map views** | People looking for food nearby |
| **Calls** | Direct calls from listing |
| **Website clicks** | Visits to your site |
| **Direction requests** | People planning to visit/find you |
| **Photo views** | Which photos attract attention |

Log these monthly in `monthly-kpi-dashboard.csv`.
