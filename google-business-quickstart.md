## Step 11: Request Verification

Google will offer verification options:
1. **Postcard** (most common for service-area businesses)
2. **Phone call or SMS** (sometimes available)
3. **Email** (sometimes available)
4. **Video verification** (increasingly common)

Choose the fastest option. If postcard, it takes 5–14 days.

**Important:** Verification must be completed before the profile goes fully live in Google Search/Maps.
