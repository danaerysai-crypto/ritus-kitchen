# Ritu's Kitchen — Marketing Plan v2
## Auroville-First: For New Crowd & Auroville People

**Author lens:** 30-year marketing veteran. One-woman home kitchen. No budget to burn. Every rupee and every hour must earn back.

---

## 1. Who Ritu's Kitchen Really Serves in Auroville

Auroville is not a normal Indian town. It is a 3,000+ person intentional community with a rotating door of visitors, volunteers, newcomers, and long-term residents. Your best customers are not "tourists." They are the people who are *staying* and want reliable, clean, home-cooked food without cooking themselves.

### Primary segments

| Segment | Who they are | What they need | Where to reach them |
|---------|--------------|----------------|---------------------|
| **Newcomers (first 0–6 months)** | Just arrived, often in a guesthouse, overwhelmed by local food options, no kitchen access | Easy ordering, English communication, familiar-but-healthy food, dietary clarity | Guesthouses, Auroville Welcome Center, newcomers' WhatsApp groups, notice boards |
| **Long-term Aurovilians** | Busy with karma yoga/work, some have kitchens but are time-poor | Weekly tiffin, subscriptions, consistent quality, trust | Word-of-mouth, community radio, WhatsApp groups, personal recommendation |
| **Volunteers & interns** | Young, budget-conscious, staying 1–12 months | Affordable regular meals, veg/vegan options, pickup/delivery | Youth camps, volunteer houses, Sri Aurobindo Ashram connections |
| **Wellness / yoga crowd** | Health-focused, often gluten-free, dairy-free, sattvic | Clean ingredients, no maida, no excess oil, customizable | Yoga centers (Santosh, Shraddha, etc.), wellness groups, organic shops |
| **Families visiting Auroville** | Parents with kids, want safe home food for children | Mild spice, reliable hygiene, thali format, pre-order certainty | Guesthouses, Matrimandir visitors, family WhatsApp groups |
| **Eco-village & farm residents** | Living outside central Auroville, limited food access | Bulk delivery, weekly meal packs, tiffin subscription | Farm WhatsApp groups, collective kitchens |

### The Auroville buyer psychology

- **Trust before taste.** People need to know the kitchen is clean, the water is safe, and the cook cares.
- **Community proof.** One strong recommendation from a respected Aurovilian beats 50 Instagram ads.
- **Simplicity over choice.** Aurovilians are decision-fatigued. Today's thali, take it or leave it, works better than a 30-item menu.
- **Values alignment.** Local, organic when possible, minimal plastic, no waste, fair price — these matter.
- **Pre-booking is normal.** Auroville runs on advance planning. Same-day impulse ordering is not the main game.

## 2. Refined Positioning (Auroville-Specific)

**Current tagline:** "Homely food, away from home"

**Better tagline options:**

1. *"Auroville's home kitchen — fresh thalis, made the same day."*
2. *"Home-cooked South Indian meals for Auroville — pre-booked, delivered."*
3. *"No restaurant. Just Ritu's kitchen, your thali, today."*

**Recommended winner:**

> **"Home-cooked thalis in Auroville — fresh today, delivered to you."**

Why: It says *what* (thalis), *who made it* (home-cooked), *where* (Auroville), *freshness* (today), and *convenience* (delivered). No vague poetry.

## 3. The Product Ladder (Built for Auroville Life)

Stop selling dishes. Sell *meal solutions*.

| Product | Price (suggested) | Who buys it | Why it wins |
|---------|-------------------|-------------|-------------|
| **Today's Thali** | ₹220 | Everyone | Decision-free, fresh, daily |
| **Newcomer Starter Pack** | ₹1,200 for 6 thalis | New arrivals | Discounted, builds habit, simple onboarding |
| **Weekly Tiffin** | ₹1,100 for 5 meals | Long-term residents | Predictable revenue for Ritu, convenience for customer |
| **Wellness Thali** | ₹280 | Yoga/wellness crowd | Gluten-free, vegan, low-oil, no onion-garlic option |
| **Family Thali (2 people)** | ₹400 | Families | Larger portions, mild spice |
| **Weekly Fridge Stock** | ₹1,500 | Busy workers | 5 pre-portioned, reheatable meals |
| **Festival / Special Box** | ₹450–₹650 | Community celebrants | Diwali, Pongal, Onam, Christmas special |
| **Event Catering** | Custom | Guesthouses, yoga centers | Bulk orders for retreats/workshops |

**Pricing principle:** Be 10–15% below comparable restaurant thali prices but communicate that this is *home food*, not restaurant food. The value is trust + freshness + customization, not cheapness.

## 5. WhatsApp-First Sales Funnel

Auroville runs on WhatsApp. Your website is a brochure. WhatsApp is where orders happen.

### 5A. Customer journey

| Stage | WhatsApp action |
|-------|-----------------|
| **Discover** | Someone shares your number or QR in a group |
| **Curious** | They message "Menu?" → auto-reply with today's menu + thali photo |
| **First order** | One thali, simple, no customization |
| **Happy** | Ask for a review/photo, add to broadcast list |
| **Repeat** | Daily broadcast: "Today's thali — reply YES" |
| **Loyal** | Offer weekly tiffin subscription |

### 5B. Broadcast templates (use sparingly, daily max 1)

**Morning menu broadcast:**
> 🍃 Good morning Auroville! Today's thali: sambar, rasam, poriyal, kootu, rice, chapati, papad, pickle + payasam. ₹220. Pre-book by 11 AM. Gluten-free and vegan options available. Reply YES + quantity.

**Evening next-day preview:**
> 🌙 Tomorrow's thali preview: [brief menu]. Pre-book tonight and lock it in. Reply YES.

**Scarcity nudge:**
> 🔥 Only 8 thalis left for today. Reply YES to reserve.

### 5C. WhatsApp Business setup

- Set business hours
- Create quick replies: Menu, Price, Location, Dietary, Weekly plan
- Set greeting message for new chats
- Use labels: Regular, New, Tiffin, Wellness, Guesthouse

|--------|----------|

| Auroville Today | "New in Auroville? Try Ritu's Kitchen" listing |

4. After delivery, message: *"Hope you enjoyed it. If you liked it, a quick Google review helps other newcomers find us. Link: ..."*
5. Add them to broadcast list.

## 14. What NOT to Do

| Don't | Why |
|-------|-----|
| Don't compete on price with Auroville cafes | You will lose. Compete on trust + home-cooked freshness. |
| Don't over-menu | Too many choices kill orders. Thali + 2–3 add-ons is enough. |
| Don't spam WhatsApp groups | One intro post + occasional updates. Respect community norms. |
| Don't use generic stock photos | Auroville people spot fakes fast. Use real photos of Ritu's food. |
| Don't ignore dietary requests | Auroville has high vegan/gluten-free demand. Be clear and reliable. |
